Webster's Dictionary Fixes Mistake in Salmon's Definition

2015/9/10

A recently discovered error in the entry for the word salmon in Webster's Dictionary has been fixed. Until last week, the world-famous dictionary had erroneously defined salmon as "a very small, river- dwelling whale".

Here is the updated definition:

Salmon
a fish
some pinkish colour

The Salmon Population is Way Up This Year!

2015/8/24

The salmon population in the Capilano River has doubled in 2015. If this trend continues, salmon will outnumber people in the surrounding area in a few years. North Vancouver's government is expected to be overthrown by the Great Capilano Salmon Horde by 2021.

Number of Salmon in the Capilano River
Year Number of Salmon
High Estimate Low Estimate
2015 500,000 400,000
2014 250,000 150,000
2013 100,000 80,000
2012 10,000 9,000
