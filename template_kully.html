Company Profile

Get Your Salmon On! is an American tour operator that operates in the Metro Vancouver Capilano area. They have earned a reputation for satisfying the needs of travelers, locals and educators with an appreciation for the marine ecosystem.

Our Mission

To provide our client's with unforgettable, enjoyable experiences that exceed their expectations while educating them about the fish habitat and how to protect it.

Our Vision

A one-of-a-kind tourism company that is recognized for their professionalism, high quality service and environmental advocacy with the main purpose of contributing to salmon fish habitat restoration projects.
