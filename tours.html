Tours

Ever since 2010, Get your Salmon On! has been providing a one-of-a-kind tour experience to educate the world about maintaing fish stocks for future generations. This unique approach to advocating for the preservation of the marine ecosystem through recreational and educational tours has made Get your Salmon On! a recognized tour operator in the Lower Mainland.
